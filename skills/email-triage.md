# Claude Skill: Email Triage

**Purpose:** Process incoming emails, categorize, prioritize, and draft responses.

**Trigger:** Files in `/Processing/` folder with type `email`

---

## Skill Definition

```
Skill Name: email-triage
Version: 1.0.0
Author: AI Employee Team
```

---

## Capabilities

1. **Read and analyze** email content
2. **Categorize** by type (inquiry, invoice, meeting, etc.)
3. **Assess priority** based on content and sender
4. **Draft responses** following Company Handbook guidelines
5. **Create action items** if follow-up is needed
6. **Flag for approval** if response requires human review

---

## Workflow

### Step 1: Read Email Metadata

```markdown
From the email file, extract:
- From: sender email and name
- Subject: email subject line
- Received: timestamp
- Keywords: any tags
- Priority: assigned priority
```

### Step 2: Analyze Content

```
1. Identify email type:
   - Inquiry (question about services)
   - Invoice/Billing
   - Meeting Request
   - Follow-up
   - Newsletter/Marketing
   - Personal
   - Spam

2. Assess sender:
   - Known contact? (check previous emails)
   - VIP client?
   - New contact?

3. Determine urgency:
   - Contains "urgent", "asap", "emergency"?
   - Time-sensitive request?
   - Deadline mentioned?

4. Identify action needed:
   - Reply required?
   - Forward to someone?
   - Create task?
   - Archive only?
```

### Step 3: Create Output

**Option A: Draft Response (for routine inquiries)**
```markdown
---
type: email_draft
original_email: [filename]
to: [recipient]
subject: Re: [subject]
requires_approval: true
---

# Email Draft

**To:** [recipient]
**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

---

Dear [Name],

[Draft response based on inquiry type]

Best regards,
[Your Name]

---

## Approval

Move to /Approved/ to send
Move to /Rejected/ to discard

---
*Generated by email-triage skill*
```

**Option B: Action Plan (if email requires work)**
```markdown
---

---

```

## Response Templates

### Template 1: General Inquiry

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for your inquiry about [topic].

[Brief answer or direction to more information]

If you have any additional questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Meeting Request

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for the meeting request.

I'd be happy to meet to discuss [topic]. Here are some available times:

- [Date/Time 1]
- [Date/Time 2]
- [Date/Time 3]

Please let me know what works best for you.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 3: Invoice Received

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for sending invoice #[number].

I've received it and it's been forwarded to our accounts payable team for processing.

Payment will be processed according to the terms ([terms]).

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## Approval Rules

### Auto-Approve (can send without human review):
- Acknowledgment emails
- Meeting confirmations
- Information-only responses

### Require Approval (must be reviewed):
- Financial commitments
- Contract-related
- New client communications
- Complaints or sensitive issues
- Anything with dollar amounts
